RSVP App

In this project, I have used React to build an app for keeping track of RSVP's. I learned how to break (refactoring) the app into components and how they should connect to each other. The app can keep track of the guest list, guests names, confirmed guests and the pending guests. The app also has a counter that keeps track of the attending, unconfirmed and total number of guests.
